US-008 · Declare morning commitment
Story

A quiet ritual: the dev signs the day.

As a developer, I want to multi-select the tasks I'll work on today and confirm my commitment in a single action — a daily contract instead of drifting.

Lives at /dev/[token], under the today's-tasks section (US-007). Three visual states cover the lifecycle of a single day; one extra state shows the empty-submission guard.

Four screens
01 · Editor Pick today's tasks No commitment yet today. Multi-select with a sticky confirm bar, live counter, and a meta line showing the editing window. Open editor → 02 · Empty submission Inline error Dev attempts to confirm with zero selections. Inline message in the confirm bar, CTA disabled, no toast or modal. Open guard → 03 · Confirmed · editable Today's plan Plan persisted. Amber-bordered panel with a "Committed" seal and an "Edit my plan" affordance. Non-committed tasks show muted below. Open plan → 04 · Locked Mid-day lock After the mid-day trigger, the plan is read-only. Lock band with glyph, desaturated chrome, and a deep-link to the check-in. Open locked view →
Acceptance criteria coverage
  • AC-1 Multi-select any non-empty subset and confirm in one action — Editor.
  • AC-2 Persisted plan is shown as "today's plan" — Confirmed.
  • AC-3 Editable until mid-day check-in — Confirmed ↔ Editor.
  • AC-4 Empty submission blocked with inline message — Empty.
  • AC-5 One commitment per dev per calendar day — Locked caps the day.
